What is a Network?

A computer network is a group of computers connected to share resources like files, internet, and hardware devices.

Types of Networks

  • LAN (Local Area Network)
  • WAN (Wide Area Network)
  • MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)
  • Wireless Networks (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth)

Network Devices

  • Router
  • Switch
  • Hub
  • Access Point
